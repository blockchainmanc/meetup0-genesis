WTF ... Blockchain?

"Blockchain is a continuously growing list of records, called blocks, which are linked and secured using cryptography"

- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blockchain

... so WTF is Blockchain?

Some facts about Blockchain

A Blockchain typically behaves like a bank ledger, recording a stream of events/transactions

Typically these are decentralised & peer-to-peer

  • Fault tolerant due to this decentralisation & peer-to-peer nature

Often Blockchain's are Immutable but this is not always the case

  • A decision taken by the chains creators
  • Chains may require corrections which is possible

Most are public, accountable and trustless in nature

Created by a consensus between the chain participants - `proof-of-work` & `proof-of-stake`

Many are programmable via smart contracts

  • Applications utilising the above features
  • e.g. Ethereum, Neo, Quantum

To summarise

So its a global public peer-to-peer, fault tolerant, programmable distrusted ledger, typically with consensus validation and accountability at its core!
